My story

I'm an intellectually curious individual who achieved a distinction in a Political Economy master's from King's College London in 2018. I then worked in, volunteered in or traveled to over 30 countries across 5 continents.

On this journey, I indulged in my passion of photography, became a freelance editor and, while proofing scientific articles, became exposed to what now fascinates me most, data science. See my work below.
